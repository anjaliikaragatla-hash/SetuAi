# SetuAI

**Bridging Citizens with Government Services Through AI**

## Overview

SetuAI is an AI-powered multilingual assistant designed to simplify access to government services for rural and underserved communities. Many citizens struggle to find accurate information about government schemes, healthcare, education, and farming support due to language barriers, fragmented information, and limited digital literacy.

SetuAI addresses this challenge by providing a simple conversational interface where users can ask questions in their preferred language and receive clear, easy-to-understand guidance.

---

## Problem Statement

Accessing government services can often be confusing and time-consuming, particularly for people living in rural areas. Information is spread across multiple portals, eligibility criteria can be difficult to understand, and language differences make the process even more challenging.

As a result, many eligible citizens remain unaware of the benefits and opportunities available to them.

---

## Our Solution

SetuAI simplifies this experience by acting as an intelligent assistant that helps users:

* Discover relevant government schemes
* Access healthcare information
* Find educational opportunities
* Receive farming-related guidance
* Navigate public services through natural conversations
* Interact in English or Hindi

The platform focuses on making government information accessible, understandable, and user-friendly.
